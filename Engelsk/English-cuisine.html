Filippino cuisine

Filippino food is delicious mix of local traditions with Spanish, Chinese, American and Malay influence.
It is very variable, often based on rice, meat (especially pork), seafood, vegetables and sweet-sour flavours.

Adobo

Adobo the most known dish. Meat (usually chicken or pork) is stewed in soy souse, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves and pepper.
