Filippino cuisine

Filippino food is delicious mix of local traditions with Spanish, Chinese, American and Malay influence.
It is very variable, often based on rice, meat (especially pork), seafood, vegetables and sweet-sour flavours.

Adobo the most known dish. Meat (usually chicken or pork) is stewed in soy souse, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves and pepper
Sour soup Sinigang made from tamarind or other fruits (for example kamia or green mango). Prepared with meat or fish and vegetables
Filippino grill Inihaw. Meat, fish or seafood are marinated and grilled over charcoal
Lechon is a whole pig roasted over a fire is the number one festive dish. It is popular dish at holidays and weddings
