    About Philippines

    The Republic of the Philippines is an island country in Southeast Asia. Manila is a capital of Philippines and Quezon is the largest city. The population of the Philippines is 112 million people.
    The country is located in the western part of the Pacific Ocean on the islands of the Philippine archipelago, which is part of the Malay Archipelago. There are more than 7600 islands, but it's impossible to determinate the exact number because of the volcanic activity, that causes new island to appear, and old ones disappear. Volcanic mountains run from north to south, and 50% of the territory is covered by forests.
    The official languages are Tagalong and English.
    Philippines is a multilingual country. Philippines has 72 national languages and dialects
    The currency is the Philippines peso.
    Philippines is the member of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN).
